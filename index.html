Intentional Growth‐ The Mistake Gap

This Week's Scriptures

  • 1 Cor 13:11
  • Philippians 4:6‐9

Sunday Sermon Pointers

  • Growth doesn't happen, growth is intentional.
  • We have to pursue growth for there are blessings that God will not give to the immature.
  • God intentionally made us&hypen; we were not spoken into existence like the rest of the creation. He took the time to form us and gave us His breath.
  • "The mistake gap-" when we are afraid of making mistake.
  • As we seek to grow, there is always a fear of the unknown that creeps in.
  • We fail to grow because of fear that we allowto grow in our hearts.
  • We become paralyzed from reaching our destiny.
  • How we grow may not be how someone else grows&hypen; let anxiety not paralyze us; we have to do away with insecurities and anxieties.
  • Only God and only God gives real guarantees.
  • God gives us chances to fix our mistakes; persistence makes us move forward.
  • Fear serves to stop us from advancing forward, and we should walk away from it.
  • Immaturity will make us waste our resources and talents.

Discussion Questions

  • Do you feel like you have to succeed in everything you do?
  • Is God enough to satisfy you even if everyone else rejects you or if you fail to reach your goal?
  • What tangible steps will you take to overcome the fear of making mistakes/failure?

Biblical Insights

  • Esther 4
  • Esther was afraid to fail, she was afrad that if she approached the king while he wasn't holding his scepter, she would be put to death.
  • She was reminded that failing to act had consequences on her part and family.
  • Fear as an obstacle to action can have far-reaching consequences.
